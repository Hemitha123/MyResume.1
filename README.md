# MyResume.1

This portfolio website is a personal resume-style webpage designed to showcase your profile, education, achievements, skills, certificates, and downloadable resume in a clear and modern format. The design focuses on a clean dark theme, bold headings, well-organized sections, and responsive layout. The profile section includes your image and an introduction, followed by visually structured boxes for education, skills, and achievements. A dedicated certificates section displays proofs of your accomplishments, with images and links arranged neatly in the center of the page. The resume is also included for direct download.

The website presents your academic background, technical abilities, and project/achievement history in a straightforward and attractive manner, making it suitable for placements, internships, and professional showcases.
